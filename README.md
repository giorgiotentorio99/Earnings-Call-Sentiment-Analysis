# Earnings-Call-Sentiment-Analysis
Do investors actually listen to a CEO’s optimism? I decided to put executive "strategic optimism" to the test. I analyzed 5 years of earnings call transcripts (Q1 2020 – Q4 2024) for three tech giants: Microsoft (MSFT), Tesla (TSLA), and Apple (AAPL). Can the emotional tone of a CEO or CFO predict stock price movements?
